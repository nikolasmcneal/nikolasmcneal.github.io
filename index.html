I am currently a second-year Ph.D. student in the Machine Learning program @ Georgia Tech, where I work with Ratan Murty. I am broadly interested in the computational principles underlying visual perception, particularly the relationship between representations from neural networks and humans. I obtained my undergraduate degree in Computer Science from The Ohio State University in 2023.
